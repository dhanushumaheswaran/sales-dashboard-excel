# Sales Performance Dashboard (Excel Project)

## Overview
This project presents an interactive Sales Performance Dashboard built using Microsoft Excel. It provides insights into sales, profit, discount impact, and regional performance to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Objective
The goal of this dashboard is to:
- Analyze overall sales and profitability
- Identify high-performing regions and product categories
- Detect areas where high discounts reduce profit
- Track monthly sales trends

---

## Tools & Techniques Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers (Interactive Filters)
- Data Cleaning and Transformation
- Custom Formatting (K/M values, percentages)

---

## Dashboard Features

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Total Sales: ₹2,297.2K
- Total Profit: ₹286.4K
- Total Discount: 15.62%
- Quantity Sold: 37,873 Units

---

## Visualizations Included

### Monthly Sales Trend
- Tracks sales performance across months
- Helps identify seasonal patterns

### Sales by Category
- Compares performance of Technology, Office Supplies, and Furniture

### Sales by Region
- Regional performance breakdown across West, East, Central, and South

### Profit by Category
- Highlights profitability differences across product categories

### Discount vs Profit
- Shows the relationship between discount levels and profitability

---

## Interactive Filters
- Region
- Category
- Year (2014–2017)
- Segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)

These filters allow dynamic and real-time analysis.

---

## Key Insights
- West region generates the highest sales
- Technology category contributes the most revenue
- Furniture category shows lower profitability
- High discounts negatively impact profit margins
- Sales increase in later months of the year

---

## Business Impact
This dashboard helps stakeholders:
- Optimize discount strategies
- Focus on high-profit categories
- Improve regional sales planning
- Make data-driven decisions
